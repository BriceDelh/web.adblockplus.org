{{s7 Spread the word}}

{{s17 While nobody profits directly from it, widespread adoption of ad blocking software will make intrusive ads economically inefficient until they become as rare as pop-up windows already are today.}} {{s18 And aside from making the internet a better place it is simply good to know that this work is used.}}

{{s19 There are many ways how you can help our cause by making Adblock Plus more popular:}}

  • {{s20 Follow us on Twitter, Facebook or Google+ and recommend ABP to all your friends.}}
  • {{s21 If Adblock Plus has improved your browsing experience, share this in your blog or on social networks.}}
  • {{s22 Convince your employer or university to install Adblock Plus.}}
  • {{s23 Approach your local media to report about privacy and security on the internet.}}

{{s8 Report bugs}}

{{s25 If you notice that Adblock Plus isn't behaving the way you think it should — tell us.}} {{s26 There are always situations we didn't test for that might produce incorrect results, we need your help to find those.}} {{s27 Please include all the information we need to reproduce the bug: under which conditions does the bug occur, which browser did you use, what was the version of Adblock Plus?}} {{s28 Also stay around for follow-up questions we might have and to confirm that the bug was really fixed.}}

{{s29 Test development builds}}

{{s30 Before a new version of Adblock Plus is released there are always several development builds.}} {{s31 The purpose is to catch bugs or even badly designed features before they are released.}} {{s32 Subscribe to the RSS feed to know when a development build is released.}}

{{s9 Contribute code}}
{{s34 Whether you're fixing a bug or adding a feature you'd like to see, code contributions are always welcome.}}
{{s35 Contributing code is more involved than contributing in other ways, but highly appreciated. There's a lot to do! Please see the contribute code page to get started.}}

{{s10 Help other users}}

{{s37 If you are an experienced Adblock Plus user maybe you can spend a little time answering questions of people who are new to this.}} {{s38 Have a look at the forum, there are always many users looking for answers.}}

{{s11 Translate Adblock Plus}}

{{s40 Currently Adblock Plus has been translated into more than 30 languages — yet some translations have been abandoned and we still need translators for more languages.}} {{s41 If you want to contribute in this way please register on Crowdin and go to our translation projects: Adblock Plus for Firefox, Adblock Plus for Chrome, Element Hiding Helper, Customizations for Adblock Plus.}} {{s42 Choose a language and start suggesting translations.}} {{s43 And if you don't want to translate just yet you can also vote on the translations provided by other people.}}

{{s12 Bring in suggestions}}

{{s45 There is always something that can be improved, so please tell us about your ideas.}} {{s46 Ideally you should not only tell us what needs to be changed (this is mostly already known) but also how it should be changed.}} {{s47 Feel free to open a new thread in the forum or join an existing discussion.}} {{s48 Note that for various reasons not every suggestion will be accepted, and please stay constructive in your criticism.}}

{{s49 Donate}}

{{s50 With more funds we will be able to improve Adblock Plus faster.}} {{s51 You can make a donation on the contribution page.}}

{{s14 Add or improve filters}}

{{s53 Adblock Plus is only as good as its filters.}} {{s54 The elements Adblock Plus blocks by default are defined in filter lists which are maintained by voluntary contributors.}} {{s55 By constantly updating them, this community ensures that Adblock Plus blocks all annoying ads on every website.}}

{{s56 If you come across a website on which an ad isn't blocked or filter lists cause issues, please report it.}} {{s57 Also, you can}} {{s58 learn how to create filters}} {{s59 yourself.}} {{s60 If you have knowledge about the filter syntax of Adblock Plus, you can suggest new filters in our forum (registration is not required, but recommended in this case).}} {{s61 Active contributors may be promoted to become part of the group of filter list maintainers.}}

{{s15 Evaluate acceptable ads}}

{{s63 Adblock Plus is the initiator of the Acceptable Ads initiative, which is aimed at supporting websites that choose to advertise using unobtrusive ads.}} {{s64 Based on}} {{s65 strict guidelines}} {{s66 everyone can join the discussion to determine which ads qualify as Acceptable Ads.}}

{{s67 If you would like to help determine which ads and new ways of advertising are acceptable, please visit the forum to share your thoughts.}}
