{{s3 Adblock Plus will run as background service and display its status in notification area.}}

{{s5 You can change subscription at any time by selecting it from a list:}}

{{s6 Each time you select a different subscription, it is automatically downloaded and activated. By default, subscriptions are being refreshed periodically. There are two other options available in the advanced settings:}}

  • {{s9 On start — the subscription is automatically refreshed when you start Adblock Plus.}}
