{{s5 Block Ads}}
{{s15 Adblock Plus blocks all annoying ads on the web by default: video ads for example on YouTube, Facebook ads, flashy banners, pop-ups, pop-unders and much more. It does this based on the filter lists you've chosen.}}
{{s16 Simply install Adblock Plus to your browser (it is available for Firefox, Chrome, Safari and Opera) or your Android smartphone or tablet and all intrusive ads are automatically removed from any website you visit.}}

{{s17 Acceptable Ads}}
{{s18 Many websites rely on advertising revenues, so instead of blocking all ads, we decided to try and encourage websites to use non-intrusive advertising instead of annoying ads people are most like to ignore and want to block.}}
{{s19 To accomplish this, we established strict guidelines with our users and third parties to identify acceptable formats and placements. These ads are therefore allowed out of the box.}} {{s20 You can always}} {{s21 disable this feature}} {{s22 if you want.}}

{{s7 Disable Tracking}}
{{s24 With every browsing session, there are multiple firms tracking your online activity and browsing history.}} {{s25 There are hundreds of ad agencies tracking your every move, but with Adblock Plus you can easily disable most tracking, and browse the web more anonymously.}}

{{s26 According to Stanford University research, the EasyPrivacy tracking protection filters are the most efficient of all available tracking protection solutions.}}

{{s27 To enable this feature, please open this dialogue and click “Add” to protect yourself against tracking. If you wish to disable this feature, please read here how to do this.}}

{{s8 Disable Malware Domains}}
{{s29 Slow startup, popups, advertisers hijacking your browser: Those are all signs of your computer being infected by malware.}} {{s30 Even worse, computers infected by malware open the doors to all kinds of cyber criminality threats.}} {{s31 These infected computers can then be used to sending out spam emails or attacking other computers or servers, stealing passwords, social security numbers, personal documents and credit card information.}}

{{s32 Adblock Plus can be configured to block domains known to spread malware, protecting your computer against viruses, Trojan horses, worms, spy- and adware.}}

{{s33 To enable this feature now, please open this dialogue and click “Add” to protect yourself against malicious domains. If you wish to disable this feature, please read here how to do this.}}

{{s9 Disable Social Media Buttons}}
{{s35 Buttons to share content on social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus and many others are placed on almost every website you visit.}} {{s36 Even if you are not clicking them, every website containing these buttons is sending a request to the servers of the social network which can use that information to create a profile based on your browsing habits.}}

{{s37 You can use Adblock Plus to remove social media buttons from every website, making sure that social networks can’t create a profile about you based on the websites you visit.}}

{{s38 To enable this feature now, please open this dialogue and click “Add” to protect yourself against Social Media tracking. If you wish to disable this feature, please read here how to do this.}}
